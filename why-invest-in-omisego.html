Why Should you invest in OmiseGo (OMG)  Righ Now?

We at CryptoEase have really taken to Omisego (OMG) in its infancy. We don’t usually deal with cryptocurrencies until they are well established because of the uncertainty that surrounds newly launched blockchain projects. OMG, however, is different. 

Omise, OMG’s parent company, is a well-established fintech company and has a solid working model already in place. They have just released their blockchain project, OMG, in July 2017 and it has really taken most of the crypto community by surprise. Unless you know of their team and the platform that OMG are going to deliver, that is.
OMG is a payment processor that will completely disrupt the financial transaction industry. It will allow you to move money across different payment systems, while at the same time making it easier for the 2 billion people, who don’t have a traditional bank account, to actually send money.
The payment processing industry moves over $3.5 trillion every year, and these payment processors are usually third-party companies who add to the charge of the payment process. Payment processing companies like Visa, Mastercard and others like, Alipay and Paypal move billions of dollars every single day, but each of these has their own closed payment ecosystem. The problem lies within these closed ecosystems - they can’t connect with each other. Therefore, if you want to send money from Visa to Alipay, you must have accounts for each one. These financial companies make it difficult and expensive so they can hold onto the fees and generate much more profit for themselves. 

OMG, who are currently on the Ethereum blockchain but intend to launch their own in Q4 of 2017, are going to be the blockchain link between these closed payment networks. They will be the platform that allows these to speak with each other; therefore, creating an interoperable payments platform, which will result in massive cost reduction. This will allow its users to move any digital assets across any payment network. 

They have a dedicated and well-established fintech team, and also a star-studded blockchain advisory team, including Ethereum founder, Vitalik Buterin. What’s more, the platform they’re using is the exact model Ethereum used to vault its market cap value from under $30 million to over $25 billion. Ethereum created a platform that opened the way for developers to build their own blockchain applications, with the advantage of just building the application and not having to build an entire blockchain. OMG are doing the same thing but with payments. With the OMG platform, developers can build payment applications without having to go through the process of setting up their own payment platform network.
You may think ‘Why will people choose OMG over traditional payment providers?’ Well, bear in mind that there are about 2 Billion people who don’t have a traditional bank account. OMG don’t require you to have a bank account to make payments. OMG will also significantly lower the costs of transactions.
