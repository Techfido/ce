IOTA in Lay Terms

Iota is a revolutionary cryptocurrency that is totally different from all the others. Iota offers the solution to blockchain’s problems. With Iota there are no scalability issues and transactions are absolutely free. That’s right, a fee-less transaction that has no scalabilty issues. What’s not to like.

BUY IOTA
Iota is a completely different ‘blockchain’ technology. In fact, it’s not even a blockchain at all. The underlying technology Iota uses is actually called the ‘Tangle’, which is a Directed Acyclic Graph (DAG). Rather than use the environmentally unfriendly proof of work system where miners complete and are paid for validating transactions, the Tangle requires all users to verify two transactions when they make one. This allows Iota to be a feeless transaction system.

Scalability issues are a serious threat to the growth of blockchain cryptos. The more people that use blockchain technology, the slower it goes. As blockchain technology grows and becomes the IT platform for business and life in general, each blockchain will constantly have to scale itself to meet demand. This has already create many problems for most cryptos, with such things as hardforks, and just slow networks. Iota will have no such issues. As I said earlier, each user must validate two other transactions. Not only will this result in feeless transactions, but it will aslo solve the scalabilty issues, because the more people that use it the faster it will go.


Iota are working with major companies like Bosch, Cisco and Microsoft in readiness for the Internet of Things (IoT) smart connections. The IoT will make things like paying for your parking fees fluent and cashless, finding the best deal to pay for your commodities or selling your products for the best available price effortless. When smart cities are growing and becoming the norm, everything will be fitted with an IoT device, and Iota will be a platform that allows these devices to talk with each other. 

An estimated 50 billion devices will be fitted and connected to the IoT in the future. That’s 50 billion devices all able to connect with each and transact with each other. Things like smart metres will be using the platform to make microtransactions to electricity companies. Our parking fees will be cashless because our vehicles will be fitted with a device that automatically pays the parking fee. And this sort of thing will be done on the IoT. 


So, a feeless platform that has absolutely no scalability issues, while allowing the transaction of micropayments from one device to the other is the main reason Iota will revolutionize even the revolutionary blockchain. I haven’t even mentioned smart contracts, but it will be launching them in 2018, and when it does, we at Cryptoease believe Iota, might not only be a successful cryptocurrency, but might possibly be the one to actually be the front runner.

People are investing big money in Iota, and it’s still in the Beta phase. Once it’s up and running, and businesses, which are just opening up to blockchain, see the potential of the Tangle will switch to Iota. Why wouldn’t they? It’s fast and free.
